About Us

Cakery Pastry, LLC brings warmth, sweetness, and artisan baking to the heart of Arden, North Carolina. Founded by Samantha McGlone, our shop blends the feel of a vintage café with the irresistible aroma of freshly baked pastries and handcrafted desserts.

Our Signature Treats

We specialize in seasonal pastries, classic cakes, gluten-free options, and locally inspired confections served daily with love and a touch of flair.
